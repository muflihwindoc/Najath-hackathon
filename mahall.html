Mahalls With Us

The Mahalls that collaborated with us have played a pivotal role in ensuring the success of our Women Empowerment Project, exceeding our expectations. Their unwavering support, dedication, and active involvement have been instrumental in achieving the project’s goals. By fostering a strong network of collaboration, they have helped us create opportunities that empower women in various spheres, including education, entrepreneurship, and social leadership.

Nadapuram Mahall

Nadapuram Mahall’s connection with the Women Empowerment Project has been instrumental in creating inspiring success stories that showcase the transformative power of collective effort and determination. Among the many achievements, two notable examples stand out.

The first success story is that of Ayisha Nusrath (ID: WEP-1023), who, with the guidance and support of the project, secured a prestigious government job in the education sector. Her journey from a determined student to a government professional exemplifies how the initiative has empowered women by providing them with opportunities for skill development and career advancement.

The second remarkable achievement is by Shahina Banu (ID: WEP-1045), who ventured into entrepreneurship and successfully established a thriving handmade crafts business. Starting from modest beginnings, her business has grown significantly, providing not only financial independence for her but also employment opportunities for other women in the community.

Vadakara Mahall

Vadakara Mahall’s collaboration with the Women Empowerment Project has been transformative, paving the way for remarkable achievements among women in the community. Among the many success stories, two stand out as shining examples of the project’s impact.

One such story is that of Naseema K. (ID: WE345), who emerged as a champion in martial arts. Through the project’s training and support, Naseema honed her skills in self-defense and martial arts techniques, going on to win accolades at state-level competitions. Her journey from a beginner to a recognized martial artist has not only inspired other women to take up self-defense but also reinforced the importance of physical and mental strength in women’s empowerment.

Another inspiring example is Shabna A. (ID: WE672), who successfully completed a vocational training program in tailoring and design offered through the project. With her newfound skills, she launched her own tailoring business, providing quality services to the local community. Shabna’s entrepreneurial journey has not only made her financially independent but also allowed her to mentor other women in similar fields, creating a ripple effect of empowerment.

Ulliyeri Mahall

The Ulliyeri Mahall’s active involvement in the Women Empowerment Project has been a transformative force, enabling many women to achieve remarkable success in various fields. Two notable examples stand out as shining beacons of this initiative’s impact.

One such success story is that of Fathima Haseena (ID: WE-1025), who emerged as a dynamic public speaker through the training programs organized under the project. Overcoming initial hesitation and self-doubt, Fathima now confidently addresses large audiences, advocating for social change and inspiring others to follow suit. Her journey from a shy participant to a celebrated speaker is a testament to the project’s ability to nurture hidden talents and foster growth.

Another inspiring achievement is that of Ayesha Rahman (ID: WE-1098), who cracked the prestigious IAS (Indian Administrative Service) examination with the guidance and support provided by the initiative. Coming from a modest background, Ayesha’s determination and hard work, combined with the mentorship offered by the Mahall, propelled her to this incredible milestone. Today, she stands as a role model for countless young women, proving that with the right support and dedication, no dream is unattainable.

Perambra Mahall

The Perambra Mahall played a vital role in driving the Women Empowerment Project, creating a pathway for several women to achieve remarkable success. Among these success stories is Fatima Nazeer (ID: WE-1023), who broke barriers to establish herself as a successful advocate. Her journey from a determined learner to a practicing lawyer has inspired countless women in the community to pursue their dreams, regardless of societal challenges. Fatima’s achievement is a testament to the project’s commitment to fostering education and professional growth.

Another shining example is Ayesha Sabeer (ID: WE-2047), who secured a prestigious position through the Kerala Public Service Commission (PSC) examination. With the guidance and support offered by the project, Ayesha overcame numerous obstacles to fulfill her aspirations of becoming a government employee. Her success not only highlights the importance of perseverance but also showcases how structured initiatives can empower women to achieve their goals. These stories of Fatima and Ayesha exemplify the transformative impact of the Perambra Mahall’s collaboration with the Women Empowerment Project.
